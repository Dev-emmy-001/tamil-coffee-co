OUR STORY

Brewing Tamil Nadu's Heritage
With Global Standards

Deep in the misty peaks of the Western Ghats, where wild orchids bloom and the air is scented with cardamom, lies the birthplace of our exceptional beans. Tamil Nadu-Based Coffee Co. was born out of a passion to honor the century-old South Indian filter coffee tradition while introducing Single Origin coffees that rival the world's best.

We collaborate directly with small-holder estate owners in Valparai, the Nilgiris, and Shevaroys, ensuring fair pay and sustainable practices. Each bean is slowly roasted to order in our state-of-the-art facility, unlocking complex profiles of chocolate, spices, and bright citrus.
